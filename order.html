Ordering rules

  • Cheesecake slices, Cheesecake Bites, Goodie Jars, and Goodie Tray: minimum 1 day prior.
  • Whole cheesecakes and Stuffed Onions: minimum 2 days prior.
  • Customers can choose any date up to 7 days ahead.
  • Time slots run from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM in 30-minute intervals.
  • Bundle savings apply on multi-jar goodie orders.
